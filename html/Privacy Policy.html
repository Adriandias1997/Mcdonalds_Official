Abans Restaurant Systems (Private) Limited Internet Privacy Policy

Abans Restaurant Systems (Private) Limited is pleased to provide information to all of its customers about its online privacy policy. Please be assured that Abans Restaurant Systems (Private) Limited uses its best effort to protect the privacy of visitors to this website.


Abans Restaurant Systems (Private) Limited only gathers personal information such as first and last names, addresses, e-mail addresses, birthday, National Identity Card number, Telephone Number, anniversary, when voluntarily submitted by you.


When you place your delivery order through our Customer Service Line 011 5 555 555, we record your conversation to ensure high quality of the service.


We do not disclose your personal information or voice recordings to any third party except in response to a valid court order and/or as required by Law, rules and regulations of any government or statutory authority.


Abans Restaurant Systems (Private) Limited may share personal information within our franchisees, our subsidiaries and our affiliates. Any use of your information by our franchisees will comply with our privacy policy.


Companies may be engaged by Abans Restaurant Systems (Private) Limited to perform a variety of functions, such as, fulfilling orders, assisting with promotions, providing technical services for our web sites, etc. These companies may have access to personal information if needed to perform such functions. These companies may only use such personal information for the purpose of performing that function and may not use it for any other purpose.


We may offer links to sites that are not operated by Abans Restaurant Systems (Private) Limited. If you visit one of these linked sites, you should review their privacy and other policies. We are not responsible for the policies and practices of other companies and any information you submit to those companies is subject to their privacy policies.


Abans Restaurant Systems (Private) Limited may change this privacy policy from time to time, when it may be necessary. We will post the revised version here.
